Frameworks Comparison
Framework Key Strengths Best For Limitations
LangChain Comprehensive tooling, extensive integrations, mature ecosystem General LLM applications, rapid prototyping Can be complex for beginners, less specialized for multi-agent systems
LangGraph Graph-based orchestration, fine-grained control, stateful workflows Complex workflows, advanced agent coordination Steeper learning curve, requires understanding of graph concepts
AutoGen Conversation-driven collaboration, human-in-the-loop support Dynamic multi-agent conversations, interactive systems Less flexible for non-conversational workflows
CrewAI Role-based collaboration, lightweight, independent architecture Structured team workflows, role-specific tasks Newer framework with smaller community and fewer integrations
