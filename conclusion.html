Conclusion
Multi-Agent Systems enable complex problem-solving through collaboration of specialized AI agents.
Different frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen, CrewAI) offer unique approaches to building agent systems.
Applications span across business, cybersecurity, finance, software development, and scientific research.
Building even simple agents requires understanding of LLMs, tools, and orchestration patterns.
