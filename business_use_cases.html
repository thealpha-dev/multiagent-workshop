Business Use Cases
Revenue Operations

Optimize sales processes, lead nurturing, and customer relationship management through automated tasks, data analysis, and personalized interactions.

Customer Support

Enhance customer service with multi-layered support where initial inquiries are handled by general agents and complex issues are escalated to specialized agents.

Supply Chain Management

Optimize logistics, inventory management, and demand forecasting by coordinating various agents representing different parts of the supply chain.

Strategic Planning

Assist in strategic decision-making by simulating different scenarios, analyzing market trends, and providing insights for business growth.
